About Us

Welcome to our Machine Learning Research Laboratory, a premier hub for artificial intelligence innovation. We specialize in groundbreaking research and development across a spectrum of AI disciplines, including Image Enhancement, Object Detection, Vision-Language Navigation, Multimodal Language Models, Large Language Models (LLMs), Optical Flow, Human Action Recognition, Sketch Recognition, and Image Enhancement, particularly in low-light conditions. Our mission is to address everyday societal challenges through AI, while also paving the way toward the ambitious long-term objective of achieving Artificial General Intelligence (AGI). Our team is dedicated to pushing the boundaries of what's possible, making significant contributions to the field of AI that benefit society at large.
